Questions

Everything you need to know.

What is a micro-influencer?+
A content creator with 1K–50K followers. Engagement rates are typically 3–5x higher than macro-influencers.
How much does it cost?+
Plans start free. Basic at 5K DZD/year, VIP at 50K DZD/year, VIP+ at 150K DZD/year.
How are creators verified?+
Manual audit: follower authenticity, real engagement, content quality, audience consistency.
Payment methods?+
Bank transfer, CCP, BaridiMob, or cash (Algiers area).
Is it free for creators?+
Yes, 100% free. No hidden fees. You get paid within 7 days of publishing.
Minimum followers?+
1,000 followers with good engagement. Quality matters more than quantity.
