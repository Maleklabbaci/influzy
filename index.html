Home/FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Influzy.

For Brands

Brand Questions

What is a micro-influencer?+
A content creator with 1K–50K followers. Their engagement rate is often 3–5x higher than macro-influencers.
How much does it cost?+
Plans start free (Gratuit). Basic at 5K DZD/year, VIP at 50K DZD/year, VIP+ at 150K DZD/year.
How are creators verified?+
We audit every profile: follower authenticity, real engagement rate, content quality, and audience consistency.
How do I pay?+
Bank transfer, CCP, BaridiMob, or cash (Algiers). Payment after brief approval.
For Creators

Creator Questions

Is it free to join?+
Yes, 100% free. No hidden fees, ever.
Minimum followers?+
From 1,000 with good engagement. Quality over quantity.
How do I get paid?+
Within 7 days after publishing. Via CCP, BaridiMob, or bank transfer.
