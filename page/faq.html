For Brands

Brand Questions

What is a micro-influencer?+
A content creator with 1,000–50,000 followers. Their engagement rate is often 3–5x higher than macro-influencers, with more authentic audience relationships.
How much does a campaign cost?+
Our plans start at 0 DZD (Gratuit). Basic at 5,000 DZD/year, VIP at 50,000 DZD/year, and VIP+ at 150,000 DZD/year.
How are creators verified?+
We audit every profile: follower authenticity (bot detection), real engagement rate, content quality, and audience consistency with their claimed niche.
How do I pay?+
Bank transfer, CCP, BaridiMob, or cash for local clients. Payment is made after brief approval and before campaign launch.
For Creators

Creator Questions

Is it free to join?+
Yes, 100% free! Sign up, we verify your profile, and you start receiving collaboration proposals. No hidden fees ever.
What's the minimum follower count?+
From 1,000 followers with good engagement. Quality over quantity — if your community is engaged and authentic, you're welcome.
How do I get paid?+
Within 7 days after approved content is published. Via bank transfer, CCP, or BaridiMob based on your preference.
